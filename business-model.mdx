## Business Unit Details

### Éxodo Corralón (Materials Supply)

#### Service Offering

The corralón business focuses on providing construction materials with professional support:

- **Permanent Stock**: Structural materials and finishes always available
- **Trusted Brands**: Curated selection of reliable manufacturers
- **Own Logistics**: Company-owned delivery vehicles for scheduled deliveries
- **Phase-Based Delivery**: Materials delivered according to construction stage to minimize on-site storage
- **Cost Optimization Advisory**: Technical guidance to purchase exactly what's needed

## How the Businesses Work Together

### Integration Points

<Steps>
  <Step title="Single Point of Contact">
    Clients can engage with one company and get services from both. A construction client automatically has access to preferential material supply.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Coordinated Deliveries">
    Construction project managers coordinate material deliveries from the corralón to arrive exactly when needed for each phase.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Technical Advisory">
    Construction team provides material specifications and quantities to corralón team, ensuring accurate purchases.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Unified Budgeting">
    Clients can receive a single comprehensive budget covering both materials and labor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Competitive Advantages

From index.html:223-227:
> "Un solo responsable: Corralón + construcción integrados para una obra sin intermediarios ni descoordinaciones."
> 
> (A single point of responsibility: Corralón + construction integrated for a project without intermediaries or coordination issues.)

**Customer Benefits:**
- Reduced coordination overhead
- Fewer payment transactions
- Single accountability for project success
- Optimized material ordering (no surplus, no shortages)
- Phase-based delivery reduces on-site storage needs

## Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Material Sales** (Corralón)
   - Retail markup on construction materials
   - Delivery fees for on-site material transport
   - Volume discounts for large projects

2. **Construction Services** (Constructora)
   - Project-based fixed pricing
   - Time & materials billing for smaller jobs
   - Service fees for specialized trades (plumbing, electrical)
   - Recurring revenue from cleaning services

### Pricing Strategy

<Warning>
  Product prices are not displayed in the catalog. The platform shows "Precio: Consultar" (Price: Inquire) for all items. This is a "quote-based" pricing model where customers request prices via WhatsApp.
</Warning>

From app.js:556:
```javascript
const priceLabel = product.price ? product.price : "Consultar";
```

**Rationale for Quote-Based Pricing:**
- Construction material prices fluctuate with market conditions
- Volume discounts negotiable
- Bundle pricing when combining materials + construction
- Competitive pricing requires market research before quoting
