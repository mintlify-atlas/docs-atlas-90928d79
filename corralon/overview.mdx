# Éxodo Corralón Overview

Éxodo Corralón is a construction materials supplier serving contractors, builders, and homeowners in the El Mollar region of Argentina.

## What We Offer

Éxodo Corralón provides a comprehensive inventory of construction materials including:

- **Structural materials**: Iron rods, steel profiles, wire mesh
- **Cement and aggregates**: Portland cement, plasticor, fine aggregate, sand, gravel
- **Masonry**: Bricks, hollow blocks, concrete joists
- **Plumbing supplies**: PVC pipes and fittings
- **Metal roofing**: Galvanized and zinc-aluminum sheets
- **Tools and hardware**: Nails, fasteners, construction tools

<Note>
The product catalog contains over 80 different materials organized into 6 main categories.
</Note>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the Catalog">
    Search or filter materials by category. View prices and units of measure.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Cart">
    Select quantity and add items to your shopping cart. Cart persists even if you close the browser.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Delivery Option">
    Toggle between delivery ("envío") and pickup ("retiro") modes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Order via WhatsApp">
    Click the WhatsApp button to send your cart as a formatted message to Éxodo Corralón.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Quote">
    Staff will respond with pricing, availability, and delivery timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Orders are processed manually via WhatsApp - there is no automatic checkout. This allows for personalized service and custom quotes.
</Note>
