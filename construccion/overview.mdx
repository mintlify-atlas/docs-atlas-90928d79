## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Consultation">
    Contact via WhatsApp or visit the office to discuss your project:
    - Project scope and goals
    - Site conditions
    - Budget range
    - Desired timeline
  </Step>

  <Step title="Site Visit & Assessment">
    Piedra Angular team visits your location to:
    - Evaluate site conditions
    - Take measurements
    - Identify challenges or opportunities
    - Discuss design options
  </Step>

  <Step title="Proposal & Budget">
    Receive a detailed proposal including:
    - Scope of work
    - Materials list
    - Timeline with milestones
    - Cost breakdown
    - Payment schedule
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contract & Permits">
    Once approved:
    - Sign construction contract
    - Piedra Angular handles permit applications
    - Finalize project start date
  </Step>

  <Step title="Construction Execution">
    Professional crews execute the project:
    - Regular progress updates
    - Quality inspections at each phase
    - Client walkthroughs
    - Problem solving in real-time
  </Step>

  <Step title="Completion & Handover">
    Final steps:
    - Final inspection
    - Punch list resolution
    - Documentation handover
    - Warranty information
  </Step>
</Steps>
