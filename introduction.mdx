## What This Platform Does

The platform serves as a digital hub for construction professionals and homeowners in Argentina, specifically serving the El Mollar region (Ruta 355). It provides:

- **Material Browsing & Quoting**: Interactive product catalog with search, filtering, and shopping cart functionality
- **Construction Service Booking**: Calendar-based appointment system for construction services
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Direct connection to business owners via WhatsApp for quotes and bookings
- **Real-Time Business Status**: Live open/closed status indicators for both businesses
- **Multi-Category Product Management**: Organized catalog covering Hierros, Cementos, Electricidad, Sanitarios, Chapas, and Herramientas

## Key Benefits

### Integrated Solution
No need to coordinate between multiple suppliers and contractors. One team manages materials, logistics, and construction execution.

### Reduced Project Delays
With synchronized material deliveries and professional scheduling, projects advance without interruptions caused by material shortages or contractor coordination issues.

### Cost Optimization
Technical advisory helps customers purchase exactly what's needed, avoiding costly surplus or emergency shortages. Transparent, detailed budgets from the start.

### Professional Quality Control
Construction services include supervision, periodic progress reports, and quality standards enforcement throughout the project lifecycle.

### Real-Time Availability
The platform provides real-time business hours tracking:
- **Corralón**: Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM - 6:00 PM
- **Constructora**: Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM - 9:00 PM

<Note>
  Business hours are tracked in Argentina/Buenos Aires timezone using the `isBusinessOpen` function in app.js:249-261
</Note>

## Platform Architecture

The website is built as a static multi-page application with:

- **Three main pages**: index.html (landing), corralon.html (materials), construccion.html (construction)
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: No framework dependencies, all functionality in app.js
- **localStorage persistence**: Cart and preferences saved client-side
- **Responsive design**: Mobile-first CSS with breakpoints for tablet and desktop
- **WhatsApp-first communication**: All quotes and bookings route through WhatsApp business numbers
